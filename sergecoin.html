What is the Sergecoin ?

Sergecoin is a cryptocurrency created and deployed on the Binance Smart Chain blockchain. It's total supply is 100 million and it has 18 decimals. Sergecoin refers to a YouTube channel named Serge. Sergecoin was inspired by the Dogecoin, Pepecoin and other memecoins. Technically speaking, Sergecoin is a token (≠coin), more precisely, a BEP-20 token as it was created for working on the Binance Smart Chain. BEP-20 tokens, and so the Sergecoins, can be kept on compatibles with Binance Chain wallets like Trust Wallet or MetaMask.
